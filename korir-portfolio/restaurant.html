Our Story

Indulge Cucina was founded in 2022 by Emmanuel Korir. What started as a small neighborhood Indulge Cucina restaurant has grown into a beloved culinary destination, known for our innovative take on traditional Kenyan dishes.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and Kenyan importers to ensure authenticity and quality in every dish. Our passion for food, family, and hospitality is at the heart of everything we do.

We believe that dining is not just about nourishment, but about creating memories and connections that last a lifetime.
